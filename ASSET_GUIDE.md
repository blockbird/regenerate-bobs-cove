## Suggested palette

These are suggested working values derived from the logo and photo set.

- Deep Teal: `#045459`
- Lime Green: `#94CD42`
- Lake Blue Accent: `#5EC7D8`
- Off White: `#F6F6F1`
- Charcoal Forest Text: `#123133`

These do not need to be exact brand standards unless the team wants to formalize them later.

## Suggested type direction

- Headings: clean, sturdy sans-serif
- Body: highly readable sans-serif
- Avoid decorative display fonts

## Image treatment guidance

- Use large crops and let photography lead.
- Avoid over-darkening the lake colors.
- Keep bird images crisp and uncluttered.
- Use gentle overlays only where text needs support.
- Preserve the turquoise water tones: they are a signature visual cue.
